# Lost Cities: Road Fixes 1.2.0

## Requirements

- Minecraft `1.21.1`
- NeoForge `21.1.235` or newer in the 21.1 line
- Lost Cities `1.21-8.3.10` exactly

## What changed

This release rebuilds interchange planning around measured site facts and the
complete compiled footprint. Compact low-gap crossings prefer diamond/SPUI-class
designs, while a stack is selected only where four real seven-block-separated
tiers fit. Stack ramps are monotonic, share their branch/merge trunks, and keep
full clearance from both mainlines.

Neighboring crossings now reserve their full approach corridors. Identical flat
shared roads can coexist; incompatible grades deterministically retain one
complete interchange and suppress the other before rendering. Custom format-2
structure levels are physical core elevations and unsafe site-specific geometry
is rejected.

The final surface is clearance-validated and world writing clears all vehicle
envelopes before placing decks and supports. `/lostcitiesroadfixes status` now
includes family counts, and `/lostcitiesroadfixes explain <chunkX> <chunkZ>`
reports the exact decision and measurements.

Root cause and test evidence are recorded in the
[compiled-corridor regression](../regressions/2026-07-compiled-interchange-corridors.md)
and [ADR-0023](../architecture/adr-0023-compiled-corridors-and-physical-tiers.md).

## Installation

1. Back up the world.
2. Replace version 1.1.1 with `lostcitiesroadfixes-1.2.0+mc1.21.1.jar` on the
   server and every connecting client.
3. Keep Lost Cities `1.21-8.3.10` installed.
4. Generate fresh chunks. Existing roads, interchanges, and buildings are not
   rewritten.
